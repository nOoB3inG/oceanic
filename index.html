About us

We are into this field since 2002. Our company provides professional shipping services to the ship owners worldwide. The principle activities include crew manning and ship agency handling various types of vessels for Indian & foreign companies. The company management and staff are fully qualified, very well experienced and having strong background and knowledge in shipping.

We supply manpower to various countries, especially for Middle East, U.K, Europe and Persian Gulf region. We have vast experience in this field.

We are committed to provide qualified, skilled and trained seagoing personnel and have cut-edge expertise in servicing the needs of our principal companies to the best of their satisfaction.
